Why Grover Beach?

Grover Beach is a small, quiet coastal town between Pismo Beach and Arroyo Grande. It's close enough to services in San Luis Obispo County but far enough from big-city triggers. Residents benefit from walkable neighborhoods, ocean air, parks, and a tight-knit community that supports recovery.
The cost of living is lower than nearby Pismo Beach or San Luis Obispo, and the pace of life helps residents focus on building routines, finding work, and staying accountable.
