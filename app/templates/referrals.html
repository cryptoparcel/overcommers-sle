What we provide

Safe, sober, structured housing with peer support, weekly meetings, life-skills workshops, job readiness, and accountability. A stable environment where your clients can build routines and follow through on their recovery plan.

What we don't provide

Medical treatment, therapy, detox, psychiatric care, or medication management. We are not a licensed treatment facility. If a resident needs clinical services, we help connect them to appropriate providers.

How to refer someone

1
Check availability

Email info@overcomersrc.com or call to ask about current bed availability and any waitlist.

2
Have your client apply

They can fill out the online application (2-3 minutes). You're welcome to assist them with the form.

3
We follow up

We contact the applicant within 24 hours for a phone screen. We'll coordinate with you as needed for care continuity.

4
Tour & move-in

If it's a fit, we schedule a tour and walk through orientation, agreements, and move-in logistics.

Typical response time: within 24 hours. For urgent placements, email with "URGENT" in the subject.
