988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 - free, confidential, 24/7 crisis support.

SAMHSA National Helpline

Call 1-800-662-4357 - free referrals to treatment facilities, support groups, and community organizations. 24/7, 365 days.

☎
National Alliance for Eating Disorders Helpline

Call 1-866-662-1235 - free support and treatment referrals.

☎
Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 - free, 24/7 crisis counseling via text.
