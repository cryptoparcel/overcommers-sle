Central Coast sober living with structure

The Central Coast offers something LA and the Bay Area can't: space, quiet, and a real community. Our homes in Grover Beach are close to Pismo Beach, Arroyo Grande, and San Luis Obispo — with easy access to jobs, meetings, and services — without the chaos of a big city.
We welcome residents from Paso Robles, Atascadero, Santa Maria, Lompoc, and anywhere on the coast who are ready for a fresh start. The Central Coast's recovery community is strong, with AA/NA meetings throughout the county, sober social events, and employers who understand and support people in recovery.

What makes Overcomers different

We're not a treatment facility. We don't charge insurance or require a clinical referral. Overcomers is peer-based sober living: clean housing, clear expectations, and real accountability. Residents contribute to the household, attend weekly meetings, submit to random drug testing, and support each other.
Every home includes furnished beds, all utilities, Wi-Fi, laundry, kitchen access, and outdoor space. We also connect residents with employment opportunities, life-skills workshops, and local recovery resources throughout the Central Coast.

Getting started

Start by reading our resident guide, then apply online or schedule a tour. We respond within 24 hours. If it's a fit, you can pay your deposit online and we'll schedule your move-in date.
We also work with referral partners including counselors, probation officers, treatment centers, and social workers. If you're referring someone, we make the process simple and transparent.
