Why Grover Beach?

Grover Beach is a small, quiet coastal town between Pismo Beach and Arroyo Grande. It's close enough to services in San Luis Obispo County but far enough from big-city triggers. Residents benefit from walkable neighborhoods, ocean air, parks, and a tight-knit community that supports recovery.
The cost of living is lower than nearby Pismo Beach or San Luis Obispo, and the pace of life helps residents focus on building routines, finding work, and staying accountable. There are AA and NA meetings within a short drive, local employers who hire in recovery, and a community that understands second chances.

What we offer

Our homes are clean, furnished, and structured. Every resident gets a bed, closet space, and access to shared living areas — full kitchen, living room, laundry, and outdoor space. All utilities, Wi-Fi, and household supplies are included.
Beyond housing, Overcomers provides weekly house meetings, life-skills development, employment connections, and a peer support network. We hold each other accountable through random drug testing, shared responsibilities, and honest conversations.

Who it's for

Overcomers welcomes men and women in early recovery, reentry, or life transition who are ready for a structured, substance-free environment. We don't require insurance, a clinical referral, or prior treatment. What we do require is willingness to stay sober, follow house expectations, and contribute to the household.
We also serve veterans, work with referral partners, and support families who want the best for their loved ones.
