Veterans Support

Structured housing and reentry support built around stability, not pressure.

Apply Schedule a tour

Transitioning out of service - or back into civilian life after treatment or incarceration - comes with real challenges. We provide a calm, structured home where veterans can rebuild routines, find employment, and reconnect with support systems at their own pace.

We are not a VA program or clinical facility. We're peer-based supportive housing that works alongside whatever care you're already receiving.

Calm rebuilding

A stable home with clear routines, respectful housemates, and no drama. Quiet hours, clean spaces, and the structure that helps you get back on track.

Reentry help

Step-by-step support with paperwork, appointments, ID renewal, job search, and daily logistics. We help simplify the transition.

Resource connections

We'll help connect you to local VA services, veteran-specific programs, recovery meetings, employment support, and community resources in SLO County.

Peer community

Live alongside others who understand recovery. Weekly meetings, shared accountability, and genuine support - not just a bed.
