What We Do

Structured, peer-based supportive housing - not a treatment center.

Apply View openings

Overcomers provides a safe, structured living environment for adults in recovery or reentry. We focus on stability: clear routines, accountability, peer support, and practical next steps - so residents can get back on their feet without the chaos.

We are not a medical program, therapy practice, or detox center. If someone needs clinical care, we help connect them to licensed providers.

Structure & routine

Clear house rules, curfews, chore rotations, and a weekly schedule. Reducing chaos and building consistency is how residents start to feel stable again.

Peer support & accountability

Residents live together in a respectful community. Weekly house meetings, check-ins, and shared goals keep everyone moving forward - one day at a time.

Life-skills workshops

Budgeting, job search, email and phone basics, time management, and practical routines. Small groups, easy language, step-by-step format.

Employment pathway

Job readiness support and opportunities through local partnerships - including Lumpia Bros Cafe in Grover Beach. Structured work experience for residents who are ready.

Lumpia Bros pathway

Reentry planning

Step-by-step help with schedules, paperwork, appointments, and getting back into the flow of life. We help residents build a realistic plan for what comes next.

Family & youth support

Simple resources and guidance for families navigating recovery together. Age-appropriate, easy-to-understand information for kids and parents.

The typical path

1
Apply

Submit a quick application. We'll review it and reach out within 1-2 business days.

2
Phone screen

A brief call to confirm fit, answer questions, and go over house expectations and pricing.

3
Move-in orientation

Review and sign house rules, confidentiality agreement, financial agreement, and resident rights. Get settled in.

4
Weekly goals & meetings

Set personal goals, attend house meetings, participate in workshops, and build routines.

5
Next steps

When you're ready, we help plan the transition - whether that's independent housing, continued support, or a new chapter.
