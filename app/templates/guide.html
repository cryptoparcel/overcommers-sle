What to expect

Structure & daily routine

You'll follow a weekly schedule with house meetings, chore rotations, curfew, and quiet hours. The goal is to reduce chaos and build consistency. Everyone is held to the same standard.

Sobriety & safety

This is a sober living environment. No drugs, alcohol, or substances on the property. We maintain a calm, respectful atmosphere for everyone. Violations are handled through a written enforcement process.

Your rights

Every resident has the right to privacy, dignity, fair treatment, and a clear grievance process. All rights are documented and shared before move-in. Read the full list.

Financial expectations

Standard pricing is $1,000/month with a $1,000 deposit. All fees are disclosed in writing before you sign anything. No hidden charges. Payment plans may be available - just ask.

What's included

A furnished bed in a shared home, utilities (water, power, trash), Wi-Fi, basic house supplies, weekly meetings, and access to workshops and peer support.

Overcomers is peer-based supportive housing - not a medical, therapeutic, or detox program. If you need clinical care, we help connect you to licensed providers.
