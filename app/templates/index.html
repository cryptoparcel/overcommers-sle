A safe, sober place to live while you get back on your feet.

Transitional sober living for recovery & reentry — with structure, peer support, life‑skills workshops, and a plan for what’s next.

Overcomers | SLE is a supportive living environment. We focus on clear house rules, respectful community, and practical steps that help residents build stability.

Life‑skills workshops are a core part of what we provide — including transformative thinking, sober living routines, job readiness (applications, resumes, interviews), social etiquette, emotional intelligence, anger management, help navigating FAFSA/college grants, and practical steps like getting a driver’s license.
