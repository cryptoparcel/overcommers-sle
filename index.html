OVERCOMMERS | SLE: Transitional Sober Living for Addiction Recovery and Prison Reentry

In alignment with California standards for supportive housing, OVERCOMMERS | SLE, owned by Lumpia Bros Cafe, offers structured, alcohol- and drug-free environments to facilitate sobriety maintenance and societal reintegration. Our programs emphasize peer support and skill development, drawing from community-oriented models to empower individuals in their recovery journey. All services comply with federal Fair Housing Act, Americans with Disabilities Act, and California Fair Employment and Housing Act requirements.

Our Commitment: Empowering Compliant Recovery and Societal Reintegration

Addiction Recovery & Rehabilitation

Pursuant to California Health and Safety Code guidelines for unlicensed supportive housing, we provide structured sobriety environments complemented by peer-led support, inspired by established community models such as those exemplified in local enterprises, to facilitate rehabilitation without offering licensed medical treatment.

Prison-to-Society Transition

In compliance with reentry support frameworks under California Penal Code, our programs assist in acclimating to contemporary societal norms, fostering environmental awareness and personal potential realization post-incarceration.

Life Skills & Technological Proficiency

Addressing the temporal disconnect from societal advancements, we offer training in essential life skills, including financial management, interpersonal communication, and navigation of modern digital systems, all within the bounds of non-therapeutic supportive services as per state regulations.

Community Give-Back and Endorsements

With affiliations to licensed professionals, certified counselors, and community stakeholders—including endorsements from local authorities, law enforcement, and civic leaders—we promote reintegration that benefits the broader San Jose community, adhering strictly to anti-discrimination laws and public benefit standards.
