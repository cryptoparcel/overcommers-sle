A safe, sober place to live while you get back on your feet.

Transitional sober living for recovery & reentry — with structure, support, and a plan for what’s next.

OVERCOMMERS | SLE is a supportive living environment. We focus on daily routines, peer support, and practical life skills. If someone needs medical or clinical treatment, we help connect them to licensed professionals.
